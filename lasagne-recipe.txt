Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds ground beef
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - One 8-ounce can tomato sauce
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon ground oregano
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - 1/4 cup corn flour
 - Cheese Sauce
 - Lasagne Sheets
 - Grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
 1. Brown the ground beef in a large bot and drain off the excess fat.
 2. Add the tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and cayenne.
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for four hours.
 4. Mix the corn flour and 1/2 cup water in a small bowl.  Add to chili.
 5. Add the beans and simmer for 10 more minutes.
 6. Make up cheese sauce if necessary.
 7. Add layer of chilli to large baking dish
 8. Cover with lasagne sheets.
 9. Cover with layer of cheese sauce
 10. Repeat steps 7, 8, 9 until there is no sauces left. Finish with Cheese Sauce
 11. Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
 12. Oven bake at 200c for 45 minutes
